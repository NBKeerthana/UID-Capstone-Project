Vetina Puppy & Kitten Milk 200g



Quantity

Vetina Puppy & Kitten Milk is a premium milk replacer formulated to meet the nutritional needs of young puppies and kittens during their crucial early stages of development. This specialized formula provides a balanced blend of proteins, fats, vitamins, and minerals to support healthy growth, immune function, and overall well-being.



  • Complete Nutrition: This milk replacer is carefully formulated to provide all the essential nutrients required for optimal growth and development in puppies and kittens, including protein for muscle development, fat for energy, and vitamins and minerals for overall health.
  • Easy Digestibility: The milk replacer is formulated to be easily digestible for young puppies and kittens, ensuring maximum nutrient absorption and minimal digestive upset.
  • Supports Immune Health: Enriched with vitamins and antioxidants, this formula helps to support a strong immune system, providing young animals with the protection they need against common infections and diseases.
  • Promotes Healthy Bones and Teeth: Contains calcium and phosphorus to support the development of strong bones and teeth, helping to prevent skeletal issues as puppies and kittens grow.
  • Convenient Powder Form: The milk replacer comes in a convenient powder form that can be easily mixed with water to create a liquid milk substitute, making feeding quick and hassle-free.
  • Suitable for Orphaned or Rejected Puppies and Kittens: Ideal for use with orphaned, abandoned, or rejected puppies and kittens who are unable to nurse from their mother or who need supplemental feeding to thrive.
  • Versatile Use: Can be used as a primary source of nutrition for young animals or as a supplement to their mother's milk if needed.
  • Trusted Brand: Vetina is a trusted brand known for its high-quality pet care products, ensuring that you are providing the best possible nutrition for your young pets.
